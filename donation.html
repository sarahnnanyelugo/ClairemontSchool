COMMUNITY SERVICE

Desiring that our school make a positive impact on the world around us, the Clairemont School has a community service requirement of 20 hours per year for all students in Grade class. Clairemont School students have been involved in such projects as assisting in the blind people school, raising funds to assist IDPS, visiting local orphanages and giving material assistance.
