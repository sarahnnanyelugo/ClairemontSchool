TUITION AND FEES

 

Invest in your child now; they will reap the benefits long into the future

We know that private, independent education is an investment in your child, now and into the future. We also know that diversity enhances our school community by bringing together differing perspectives and life experiences into our classrooms. We work with families to make Clairemont tuition and fees as affordable as possible. 

Clairemont School runs A Value Added Teaching (VAT) As a private independent school, our school is financed by tuition as well as charitable contributions. We deliberately try to keep our school fees as low as possible while ensuring an outstanding educational experience. Please contact the Admissions Office so that we may help with any questions about the admissions process, tuition and fees.
