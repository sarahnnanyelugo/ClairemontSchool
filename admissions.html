Admission

 

Eligibility & Process

Clairemont School is a Christian school. We however do not expect every family to profess Christ in order to enrol their children in the school. Children are accepted for admission based on an evaluation of previous grades and behavior, admission testing, and a mandatory pre-enrolment interview with parents is conducted with the understanding that our founding principles are on Christian values and belief. .

 

AGE CONSIDERATION

Pearls: 1year 4months to 2years 4months
Crystal: 2years 4months to 3years 4months
Nursery 1: 3years 4months to 4years 4months
Nursery 2 : 4years 4months to 5years 4months
Grade 1 to 6: 5years 4months to 12years
Parents whose Children do not meet the age cut-off will be required to:
  • Provide documentation supporting their child’s academic achievement
  • Provide a recommendation from a previous teacher
  • Submit a complete readiness checklist that is satisfactory. In addition, the child must undergo a formal assessment of readiness and academic skills.
  • Final decision to admit the child lies with the school management.
 

WAYS TO OBTAIN FORM

Please follow these steps to secure a place for your child at Clairemont:

  • Purchase an application form of  N5,000 (Five thousand Naira only), fill and return to the admissions office. Forms are to be submitted alongside the following documents:
  • 2 recent passport photographs
  • Immunization Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Last School Report and Transfer Certificate (where applicable)
  • Upon submission and confirmation of application forms, an entrance assessment date will be scheduled for the child(ren).
  • After the assessment , a parent/guardian interview will be conducted to discuss assessment, class placement and general expectations.
  • After the full round of assessments and interview, a letter of provisional offer of admission and bill will be forwarded to the parent.
  • A non-refundable fee of N20,000{ twenty thousand Naira} is to be paid within a period of 2 weeks after receipt of the provisional offer of admission. This would constitute a formal acceptance of your child(ren)’s place.
  • In line with statutory requirements, we also consider the ages of children before they are placed in their respective classes. These measures are put in place to ensure that each child thrives in all aspects of their learning and development.

    • Please note that in the event that there are a limited number of spaces available for admission, we will give you priority if:

  • Your child has siblings in Clairemont School.
  • You are able to give an assurance that your child will remain at Clairemont School for at least 4 years and will not leave at an early age.
  • Signing of the parent/school agreement and consent form.
