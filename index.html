MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR

Welcome to Clairemont School!
We are a family school that offers holistic education to parents seeking a technology driven, 21st century education for their children. We are lifelong learners who belong to a community where all individuals are valued and supported on their journey to self-discovery. At Clairemont School, we are committed to giving our pupils a solid foundation and an excellent start to life. Hence, we encourage and guide them to become independent, confident and responsible members of the community.

Life at Clairemont begins at our Pre-school and continues through to Grade 6. We utilize a curriculum that excites and stimulates children’s creativity and entrepreneurship. Clairemont family comprises of professional staff that work assiduously to provide a happy, supportive and purposeful environment for all our pupils.

I am honoured to lead a team of educators that are committed to child empowerment and wholesome development. The style of teaching enables pupil led classrooms enhanced by outdoor learning, extracurricular activities, competitions and other events.

Here at Clairemont, no child is left behind in the learning process because every child matters. This belief is an intrinsic component of our community and we ensure that all pupils get the best academic and aesthetic tutoring needed for every stage of their development.

We are always happy to discuss your child’s progress, education and welfare at any time during the school year. We educate. We Love. We Nurture. Clairemont School is truly an amazing privilege for the child you love. I look forward to meeting you as you take the all-important decision to enrol you precious child with us at Clairemont School Lagos Visit us today. We wait to welcome you.

Mrs. Oby Awugosi.
Educational Director. .
