SOCIAL ACTIVITIES

Extending fun and learning beyond the school day In the Elementary School, students often participate in extracurricular activities that take place both before and after school.

There are four sessions of activities that change depending on the session and several that last throughout the school year. Clairemont staff lead many of the activities, but some may be taught by non-school staff. We purposefully keep fees low so as many students as possible may participate. .
