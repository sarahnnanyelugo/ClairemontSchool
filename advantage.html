LEARNING RESOURCE CENTRE

The school has a Learning Resource Centre, where both staff and students serve as volunteer tutors and students can have access to educational resources online and in the library. There is also a Bridge of Care programme where students are encouraged to reach out to the less privileged in the society. We hope that all students educated in the school would have developed a love for learning that would last all through life. We also foster a family spirit that will bring about the unity that transcends all religions, ethnic groups, economic and social divisions.

 

SPORT ACTIVITIES

Louisville Girls High School is constructing a State of the Art Sports Complex to cater to the sporting and leisure needs of our students.
