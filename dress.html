HOW WE DRESS

The dress code uniform underscores the dignity and seriousness of purpose with which we regard our education. Students are expected to arrive at school each term wearing the approved school uniform.
All students must come each day in clean and neat school uniforms..

The Approved School Uniforms include:

Light blue blouse on Navy Blue skirts with the school tie
