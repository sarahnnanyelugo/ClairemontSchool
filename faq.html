  • DOES CLAIREMONT SCHOOL HAVE A HIGH ACADEMIC STANDARD?
    • Yes, we emphasize the highest academic standards obtainable globally and our external examination results attest to this. We have 100% pass rate with our pupils getting into the best secondary schools in Nigeria and abroad.
  • WHAT IS THE AGE QUALIFICATION FOR ADMISSION INTO CLAIREMONT?
    • We admit students between 1Year and 12 years old once the admission procedure is followed.
  • CAN SCHOOL FEES BE PAID IN INSTALLMENTS?
    • Parents are encouraged to pay their ward’s tuition on or before the first day of resumption. Where due to special circumstances this cannot be done, we permit payments in instalments; however same must be accompanied with cheques on dates proposed and agreed upon with the school administrator.

  • HOW CAN I PURCHASE THE ADMISSION FORM?
    • You can purchase it at the Admissions office for a fee of N5,000 or download from the school’s website.
  • IS THERE A DISCOUNT FOR SIBLINGS?
    • Yes. There is a 5% discount on tuition fees for the 3rd or more siblings.
