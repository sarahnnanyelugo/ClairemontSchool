CLAIREMONT ALUMNAE

The Alumnae & Development Office is responsible for promoting interaction between the Alumnae, the school, and with one another. We urge all alumnae to update their information in our online database. This will enable the school and the graduates to keep in touch. It is our hope that we will be able to encourage our graduates to contribute to the school through events like career days and job openings.
