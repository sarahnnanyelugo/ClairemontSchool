GET INVOLVED

There are lots of ways to connect with classmates, fellow alumni, teachers, and current students. Whether you are near or far, your legacy as a student will remain strong. We love to know about your awesome achievements. Whatever you’re up to, we hope you will stay in touch:

Stop in and see us!

Come by the school and let us know what you’re up to! It’s a good idea to touch base and be current about what’s going on at the school on the day you would like to visit. Visiting at the end of the school day is your best chance of seeing past teachers and having some time to catch up. School ends at 2:00 PM in the EYFS Section , 3:00 PM in the Grade Schools.

 

Join the Alumni Group on Our Social Media Handles

Send a request to join our Clairemont alumni group, Clairemont School Alumni. We post our upcoming events, recent happenings at school, photos from the glory days, and photos of alumni who have visited us recently at school. For daily news from the school, follow the Clairemont school Lagos Facebook page and  Instagram.

    •  

    Catch up on the latest news at the next Alumni Gathering

    We celebrated the end of another beautiful Year in December of every year. We love seeing alumni and teachers reconnect. We usually do not set a firm date but each year’s holiday bash typically holds a few days before December 25.  Every year’s bash gets better and better!

    DONATE TO CLAIREMONT

    At Clairemont School, we remain grateful for the continual support and gifts from families past and present, alumni, friends, staff, and foundations. This has enabled us to provide an unparalleled education based on our Five Pillars: Academic Excellence, Community and Service, Leadership by Example, Strong Moral and ethical values. Honesty and simplicity. We celebrate the many Clairemont families, Friends, philanthropists and Charity organizations who have contributed time, expertise, and resources to our school. The daily experiences of our students are enhanced by your generosity and kindness.

    ALUMNI GIVING

    You recognize the value of an education at Clairemont School. You understand how classroom conversations, outdoor adventures, classmates, and teachers shaped who you are and your perspective.

    If you are looking for opportunities to pay  it forward to the next generation, please consider the following:

    • Make a donation to the Annual Fund
    • Volunteer to help with or donate to our annual Fund A Need initiatives
    • Contribute to a specific programs and needs
    • Share your expertise or business connections with an in-kind gift to the school
    • Include the school in your planned giving
    • Make a gift of stock
    • Contribute to our endowment
    play it forward. If you have questions about alumni giving at Clairemont please email clairemontschool@gmail.com. The Director of Development will reach out to you.

    Thank you for making it possible for future generations to explore and learn having  the benefit of a Clairemont School Education.
